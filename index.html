These are historical June averages, not live forecasts — your trip is too far out for real-time data. ⚠️ Safety Reminders

Never turn your back on ocean waves at black sand beaches (sneaker waves are deadly). · Always check weather at vedur.is before heading out. · Register your trip at safetravel.is for remote areas. · Headlights on at all times while driving (required by law). · Stay on marked paths at geothermal areas — boiling ground can crust over.
